Omar’s Plumbing Tricks

Learned these the hard way—hope they help you out:

  • Know Where Your Water Shut-Off Is - In case a pipe bursts and your basement’s turning into a swimming pool!
  • Grease or Oil in the Sink? - Don’t do it, dump it in a can instead!
  • Winterize Your Hose Bibs - If it has an individual shut-off, turn it off or insulate it with a cover!
